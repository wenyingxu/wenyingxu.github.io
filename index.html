About me

I received the B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees in mathematics from Southeast University, in 2010 and 2013, respectively, and the Ph.D. degree in mechanical and automation engineering from the Chinese University of Hong Kong, under supervision of Prof. Jun Wang in 2016. Afterwards, I worked as a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong, from June 2016 to December 2016.

Prospective students

I am always looking for self-motivational students who are enthusiastic about distributed optimization and learning algorithms, game theory, networked systems, etc. Send me your CV if you have interest to join my research group.
